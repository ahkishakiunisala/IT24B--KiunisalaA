Selena Gomez's Autobiography

Early Life

Birth and Family

Selena Marie Gomez was born in Grand Prairie, Texas, United States on July 22, 1992 (age 32 years).

Growing Up

Her parents divorced when she was five years old, with Selena staying with her mother. Gomez had a close relationship with her grandparents as a child, who said that they "raised her" up until she found success in the entertainment industry. She appeared in various pageants while she was growing up.

High School Career

Selena Gomez went to Danny Jones Middle School in Texas and was homeschooled for high school. She earned her high school degree in 2010. She started homeschooling when the Disney show "Wizards Of Waverly Place" started picking up. Since then she's focused on her acting, music, and modeling career.
